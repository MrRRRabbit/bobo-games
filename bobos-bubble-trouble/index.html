Bobo's Bubble Trouble 🦞

Save Bobo from the bubble bath!
Score: 0 | Level: 1

How to Play:

  • Click/Tap to shoot the bubble
  • Match 3+ same color bubbles to pop them
  • Clear bubbles before they reach Bobo!
  • Chain reactions give bonus points
  • Game ends when bubbles reach the bottom
